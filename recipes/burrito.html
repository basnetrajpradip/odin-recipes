Burrito

Description

A burrito is a dish in Mexican and Tex-Mex cuisine that took form in Ciudad Juárez, consisting of a flour tortilla wrapped into a sealed cylindrical shape around various ingredients. The tortilla is sometimes lightly grilled or steamed to soften it, make it more pliable, and allow it to adhere to itself.

Ingredients

  • 2 low carb whole wheat tortillas
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons cream
  • 1 pinch salt and white pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon cumin powder
  • ½ tablespoon butter
  • 1 ½ tablespoons salsa, chunky style
  • 2 ounces Cheddar cheese, shredded

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, beat the eggs with the cream, salsa, cumin and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  2. In a non stick skillet over medium-high heat, melt the butter and then scramble the eggs.
  3. Just before they are set, add the cheese and fold-in until it begins to melt.
  4. Lay the tortillas on a flat surface. Divide the eggs equally between the two and place on the bottom third of each tortilla.
  5. Fold the bottom third over tucking in. Fold in the two sides and then roll up. Serve.
